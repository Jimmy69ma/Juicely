How we started


We started in the beginning of schoolyear 22-23 to design a business for our integrated test. We chose to create a business that makes your life easier without spending too much money. Here comes the first name: Juicely.

Basically you have a drinking bottle and a grenadine capsule, you put the grenadine capsule in your drinking bottle and now you have your fresh water with a little taste of grenadine coming in your mouth. So basically your water stays water but you still have the taste of grenadine.

Our process


Once we chose our business name we wanted to choose a logo that is simple so you could recognize it everywhere. We chose to add droplets underneath our business name/logo so it reminds you of the grenadine. Then we created a prototype off our drinking bottle and grenadine capsules. We also made pictures of that prototype to give us an idea if the product is good looking. We also began to work on an idea of making an advertisement video.

How we work with u


Now we work together with you, we sell you our products and you give us an idea of what you thought of it by letting a comment on our social medias. We also launched our website that is fully responsive for smartphone screens and we're still not done, next year we will evolve a lot more than we did this year.

See you next year...
